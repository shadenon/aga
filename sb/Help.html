Frequently Asked Questions

if you are new to our website click on the Create Account that is located at the bottom of the Login page then proceed to fill in the fields with the appropriate information, proceed to click on the Create button to create the account. the system will direct you to the verification page to verify your email address. please check your email and type in the field the verification code that has been sent.

if you have forgotten your password, please click on the “Forgot password” icon that is on the bottom of the Login page. you will be directed to the “Reset password” page to reset your password. make sure the password is at least 8 characters long and contains the appropriate characters. then click on the “Reset password” button. the system will send a reset password notification to notify about the new changes that have been made.

if you want to make changes to your profile please proceed to click on the profile icon, then click on the “Edit profile” button, you will then be able to change and update your profile. make sure to save the changes you have made by clicking on the “save” button that is on the bottom of the page.

- to send a message to your teacher/classmate you can start by selecting the “Messages” icon that is located at the bottom of the main page of the website. you can search for your teacher/classmate username on the search bar and then click on the search button. your teacher/classmate profile icon then will appear, click on the profile to start messaging. - to write an email to your teacher/classmate go to the teacher/classmate profile to find their email listed in the contact information field. cope the email address. then email the teacher/classmate through the appropriate email app depending on the written email address.

if the issue is not included in the FAQ page please report the issue by contacting customer services through our email address.
